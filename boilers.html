Boiler options

Boiler models, controls and warranty options, explained properly.

The best boiler is not just the one with the biggest number on the box. We look at the property, hot water demand, heating load, controls, flue route, existing pipework and the warranty terms that apply. As Ideal Accredited installers, we can advise on Ideal Logic, Logic Max and Vogue options, while still giving practical advice for the wider boiler job.

L

Ideal Logic

A strong everyday boiler choice for many homes. Logic boilers are compact, practical and well suited to reliable replacement work where the property needs straightforward heating and hot water.

LM

Ideal Logic Max

Logic Max packages are designed around longer warranty options when installed, registered and serviced in line with Ideal's current terms.

V

Ideal Vogue

Vogue is Ideal's more premium range, with a higher specification feel and long warranty options for customers who want a more refined boiler package.

Warranty

Boiler warranties can run from 10 to 12 years on eligible installs.

Warranty length depends on the boiler manufacturer, model, package, installer status, registration and ongoing annual servicing. We will explain what applies before installation so you know what is covered and what needs to be maintained.

  • Correct model selected for the property
  • Gas Safe installation and commissioning
  • Manufacturer registration after installation
  • Annual servicing to protect the warranty
  • Clear handover on controls and aftercare

Ideal Halo

Better boiler control with Ideal Halo.

Ideal Halo controls help customers manage heating more comfortably with programmable schedules, simple temperature adjustment and cleaner control over when the heating runs. Depending on the Halo setup, smart app control and connected features may also be available.

  • Simple heating schedules
  • Temperature adjustment from the room control
  • Boost and override style control for everyday use
  • Cleaner matching with suitable Ideal boiler installations

Servicing

Annual servicing protects performance, safety and boiler life.

Servicing helps identify wear, safety issues, poor combustion, leaks, pressure problems and early component faults before they become expensive. It also helps keep the boiler efficient and supports manufacturer warranty requirements.
