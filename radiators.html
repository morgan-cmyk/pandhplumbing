Radiator sizing

Radiators should be sized to suit the room, not guessed.

We use BTU calculations to help match radiator output to each room. Room size, insulation, glazing, outside walls and ceiling height all affect how much heat is needed, especially when upgrading to vertical or designer radiators.

Underfloor heating

Underfloor heating pipework installed with care before the floor is finished.

MagnaCleanse

Chemical heating flushes for tired or uneven heating systems.

Sludge and debris can restrict flow, leave radiators cold at the bottom, make boilers work harder and reduce system efficiency. A MagnaCleanse chemical flush helps clean the heating circuit and remove magnetic debris from the system.

  • Useful for cold spots on radiators
  • Helps improve heating circulation
  • Can support boiler protection and system efficiency
  • Works well alongside magnetic filter installation
  • Recommended before some boiler upgrades

Infrared camera checks

Thermal camera technology helps show radiator temperature patterns.

We can use infrared camera checks to view radiator heat distribution before and after system work. This helps show cold spots, poor circulation and whether heat is moving more evenly through the radiator.
