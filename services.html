What we cover

Our main services

General Plumbing

We carry out general plumbing work including leaks, pipework, taps, toilets, sinks, cylinders, water feeds, waste pipes and planned maintenance.

Heating Systems

Heating repairs, upgrades, radiator work, controls, pipework alterations and system improvements for homes, landlords and businesses.

Boilers

Boiler-related plumbing and heating work completed to a high standard. P&H Plumbing and Heating Limited are Ideal Accredited.

Gas Safe Work

Gas work is carried out by a Gas Safe registered company. Gas Safe Registration Number: 561480.

Bathrooms & Refurbishment

Bathroom plumbing, fixture changes, pipework, first fix and second fix support for refurbishments and renovation projects.

Commercial & Domestic

We work with homeowners, landlords, developers, builders and commercial clients from Derby and across the UK.
